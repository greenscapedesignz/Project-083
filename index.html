The Strategic Deficit
🚗
Derelict Vehicle Encroachment
Commercial garages have converted the public right-of-way into informal vehicle storage along the park edge and neighbourhood roads, destroying pedestrian access.
🌊
64 Crore Litres of Lost Rainwater
Our rajakaluve and stormwater drains are blocked and concretised. Every year, 640 million litres of rainwater floods our streets instead of recharging our groundwater.
🐟
Lake Degradation & Sewage Inflow
Untreated sewage flows directly into the Tavarekere lake. Its concrete edges sever ecological connectivity, resulting in a collapse of biodiversity.
The Vision

Project 083 transforms the 850-metre Tavarekere park edge into a resilient, pedestrian-first, ecologically functional public realm. It serves as the Koramangala Valley's premier blue-green-grey-social corridor, demonstrating real-world rajakaluve rejuvenation and lake bioengineering.
