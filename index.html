DPR Components (v4)

Expand to view the detailed scope of work for each project component.

GIS mapping and systematic removal strategy for street de-cluttering.

Development of 3.5m continuous pedestrian walkways and Non-Motorized Transport lanes.

Integration of Sustainable Drainage Systems (SuDS) and groundwater recharge mechanisms along the stormwater drain.

Strategic placement of bins and enforcement of litter control protocols.

Native planting and layered habitat creation to support local bird and insect ecology.

Implementation of CPTED (Crime Prevention Through Environmental Design) principles with solar LED installations.

Budget validation and policy frameworks for extending the corridor interventions across the wider watershed.

Public realm design utilizing the GDHI (Greenscape Designz Happiness Index) to measure spatial comfort and emotion.

Digital integration for real-time tracking of ecological and structural performance.

Long-term framework for up-keep, funding models, and community stewardship.
